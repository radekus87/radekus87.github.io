PRIVACY POLICY - KID 24



Application provides a service that allows parents to improve their children's behavior.

Application collects the following types of information:

1. Registration and profile information.

1.1. When you add a member to your account, we collect the following information:
- Username of account member.
- Profile photo (Avatar) of account member (if user selected it). To install the avatar, the account member must grant access to the files.

How we may use it:
- To personalized your profile so you can be recognized.

We do not share this information with anyone.

2. Information on children's behavior.

2.1. When you write down your children's behavior, we collect the following information:
- The amount of good behavior.
- The amount of bad behavior.

How we may use it:
- So that you can view your children's behavior report.

We do not share this information with anyone.

3. Information of your device.

3.1. Information about smartphone or tablet. We may collect the following information:
- Unique ID smartphone or tablet for the purposes to identify your device in a unique way.
- Unique Google Ad ID.
- Device type.
- Mobile device operating system brand and model.
- IP addresses.

How we may use it:
- To monitor and improve our Service.

We may share your information with:
- Google Ireland Limited (AdMob), Google Inc. for the purposes of analysing information related to the Service crashes and due to the performance of interest based advertising.

4. Ads displayed in application.

4.1. For advertising purposes, which may include 3rd party advertising. In order to keep our Service free for most users, we generate revenue through trusted data partnerships. We collect the following information:
- Information about your location.
- Information of your device (point 3).

How we may use it:
- To display advertisements relevant to your interests.

We may share your information with:
- Google Ireland Limited (AdMob), Google Inc. due to the performance of interest based advertising.

5. Information that helps us improve the application.

5.1. For the purposes of analysing information related to the Service crashes. We collect the following information:
- Information about your location.
- Information of your device (point 3).

How we may use it:
- To monitor and improve our Service.

We may share your information with:
- GOOGLE Play Services
- Firebase Analytics.

6. Payment and Transaction Information.

6.1. In-app purchases (e.g., for our premium version subscription). We collect the following information:
- Information about your credit card.
- Billing address.
- Information about your credit card.
- Payment information.
- Other information such as payment time and date.

How we may use it:
- To check the validity of the subscription and make all the features of the premium version available to you.

We may share your information with:
- GOOGLE Play Services
